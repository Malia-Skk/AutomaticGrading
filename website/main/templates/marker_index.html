Dear participant,
Thank you for taking time to participate in this study. We are working on a project about automatic grading of free text answers. To this end, we wish to collect data regarding students answering questions in online assessments. If you are the age of 18 or above, fluent in English, and have some experience marking at university level, you are invited to participate in this study.
Do note the following:

  1. Your participation in this study is voluntary. We will only include you in the study if you give us consent to do so.
  2. You can discontinue with the survey at any point without adverse consequences.
  3. No personal questions or identifying particulars will be asked.
  4. This survey will be dealt with confidentially.
  5. The original survey will be stored in a secure location, in a secure database for a period of five years after which it will be destroyed.
  6. Electronic datasets will be completely anonymised and stored for an indefinite period on a password protected computer.
  7. Only the researchers involved in the project will have access to the raw data.
  8. The dataset may possibly be utilised in future research, the same confidentiality will be applied in that instance.
  9. If you have any concerns or questions related to the study in general, or the items in the questionnaire, please contact the project leader, Mr. Siyabonga Mhlongo at siyabongam@uj.ac.za, +27 11 559 1177.
